Introduction

The 2006 NFL Draft Class is undoubtedly one of the best ever. Player performance across the board demonstrates an excellent draft class at almost every position group. No. 1 pick, Mario Williams, makes a redeeming career after underperforming his rookie season. Williams records 14 sacs to reach #3 in overall rankings. Other noticeable key players for their respective teams are Brandon Marshall, Jay Cutler, Reggie Bush, Joseph Addai, Maurice Jones-Drew or AJ Hawk. Even late round picks and free agents such as Marques Colston, coming from a I-AA college like Hofstra, the 252nd of 255 picks has reached the starting WR spot in the New Orleans Saints. In his two seasons, he has caught for more than 2,200 yards and 19 TDs.
